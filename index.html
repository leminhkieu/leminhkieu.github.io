  • I am currently a Research Fellow at the University of Leeds, United Kingdom. I will join the Auckland University, New Zealand from January 2020.
  • My research focuses on incorporating Machine Learning with individual-based simulation to solve complex urban transport problems. I believe that this combination will revolutionise Transportation Science and support policymakers to give better informed decisions.
  • I am currently the Research Team Leader for a team working on an ERC Horizon 2020 project on incorporating real-time Big Data into large-scale Agent-Based Simulation model of cities. My other research experience includes being the PI for a project for the Alan Turing Institute and being the Co-I for a proposal for funding from the British Academy. \newline Previously, I led a small team to deliver the prestigious NSW Premier's Innovation Initiative project at the CSIRO, Australia.
