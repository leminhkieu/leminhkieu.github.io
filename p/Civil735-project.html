What is Exploratory Data Analysis?

Exploratory data analysis (EDA) is an approach to analyzing and visualizing data to gain insights and understanding of the underlying patterns and trends in the data.

Gain a better understanding of the data and identify potential insights and hypotheses for further investigation.

Common EDA techniques include creating histograms, scatterplots, boxplots, and density plots to visualize the distribution of data, calculating summary statistics such as mean, median, and standard deviation, and using correlation matrices and heat maps to explore relationships between variables.
