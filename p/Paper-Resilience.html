What's in this research?

We introduce four new indices – Risk Perception, Experience, Impact, and Resilience – to provide a visual global comparison of individuals' concerns, exposures, and preparedness towards major personal harms and disasters across the globe

Where is New Zealand?

We introduce a causal discovery and inference analysis to shed lights into the underlying dynamics that shape resilience at a global level
