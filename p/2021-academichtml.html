How to get a head start in the job application process

1. Publications

2. Networking

3. Prepare your CV, Response to Selection Criteria and Cover Letter

4. Get to know the discipline you are applying to

1. Publications: How many is enough?

Depends on the quality

Sole-author papers

Think about maximising your citations early

2. Networking: How to become know

Write (at least one) strong paper(s)

Go to conferences, organise events, chair panels

Stop talking about yourself

Use social media, personal website

Involve in service roles

3. Prepare your CV: How to get an interview

Use an attractive CV template

Proofread your CV and application documents (x10 times!)

It's not about you, mate

Send to as many jobs as you would take

Involve in service roles

4. Get to know the discipline

Spy on people at the Department you are applying to!

Don't just mention names

How to hit the ground running once in the role

1. Collaborate internally first

2. Take service roles

3. Make sure you are still publishing

4. Learn new skills
