New Level 9 postgraduate course at the Faculty of Engineering from 2023: Systems Engineering for Smart Cities

Why: External drivers from the Engineering industries

Transform urban environments by learning the needs of inhabitants and improving the management of elements and services in cities

Improve resilience in cities to deal with climate change and civil emergencies

Engineering is often exact science, but future of cities are uncertain

Students bring their own unique experiences to class

We need a new course with new teaching methods where:

Construct new knowledge from existing knowledge (constructivism)

Learn from others (learning by teaching)

Draw knowledge from various disciplinaries

Learning outcomes:

Understand the complexity and challenges in urban systems in New Zealand

Evaluate the relevance of smart cities initiatives to the New Zealand context

Create a new idea for a future Smart Cities initiative to solve specific challenges in our cities
