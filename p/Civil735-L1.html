[WHY DATA SCIENCE] Now, you might be wondering, why data science? Well, let me tell you, data is everywhere. From the smartphones in our pockets to the satellites orbiting our planet, we are generating and collecting data at an unprecedented rate. And with that data comes the power to make informed decisions, solve complex problems, and drive innovation. [APPLICATIONS] Data science has applications in almost every field you can think of. In healthcare, it helps us predict and prevent diseases. In finance, it helps us make smarter investments. In sports, it helps us analyze performance and develop winning strategies. And in politics, well, it played a significant role in my own campaigns. [JOB OPPORTUNITIES] The demand for data scientists is growing rapidly, and with it comes the opportunity for high-paying jobs and fulfilling careers. By learning data science, you not just investing in your future, but also in the future of our nation and the world. [EMPOWERMENT] Data science empowers you to be a critical thinker, to question assumptions, and to make sense of the complex world we live in. It gives you the tools to become an agent of change, to challenge the status quo, and to make a real difference in your community and beyond.

What is a Jupyter Notebook?

It's a single document where you can run code, display the output, and also add explanations, formulas, charts, and make your work more transparent, understandable, repeatable, and shareable.
