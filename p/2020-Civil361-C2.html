Electronic Toll Collection

*** Electronic toll collection (ETC) is a wireless system to automatically collect the usage fee or toll charged to vehicles using toll roads, bridges, and tunnels. It is a faster alternative replacing toll booths, where vehicles must stop and the driver manually pays the toll with cash or a card. In most systems, vehicles are equipped with an automated radio transponder device that is connected to a roadside toll reader to register the vehicle's use of the road, and an electronic payment system charges the user the toll. A major advantage is the driver does not have to stop, reducing traffic delays.
